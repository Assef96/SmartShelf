Smart Shelf Project

With the advent of smart systems in the future, we will see stores full of automated systems. One of the ideas in this regard is the smart shelf of the store, which can help customers to buy products and help the store staff to monitor and control the condition of the goods on the shelf. The shelf is equipped with a number of sensors to detect the status of goods and environmental conditions, lighting system and audio player and customer guide display. Connecting this system to the IoT network allows the shelf status to be managed by a web application. In this article, a smart shelf is introduced and all the steps that have been done to design and build it are mentioned. Shelf performance is also examined from various aspects such as instrumentation, electronics, computers, networking and mechanics. We will see that it is possible to build a prototype of such a system with low cost and acceptable performance, but at a higher cost, an industrial sample can be obtained.
Key Words: Smart shelves, instruments, electronics, web applications
